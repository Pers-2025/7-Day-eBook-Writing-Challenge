Day 0: Setup and Market Research

Congratulations on beginning your 7-day eBook writing journey! Before writing, we’ll set up your environment, train ChatGPT to match your voice, and validate that your eBook concept has market demand.

Part 1: Market Validation (60 minutes)

Step 1: Analyze Bestselling eBooks

Study what’s already selling in your niche. This reveals patterns, expectations, and opportunities.

You are an eBook Market Research Specialist with 15 years of experience in digital publishing.
I want to create an eBook about [your topic].

Please:
1. List top 5 bestselling titles + key selling points
2. Analyze sales-driven language in their descriptions
3. Note price, page count, and bonuses
4. Highlight market gaps
5. Provide trends + positioning advice

Step 2: Evaluate Commercial Potential

Use this prompt to analyze your idea’s chance of success before writing:

You are a Digital Product Launch Strategist. Based on our research:

1. Rate my idea 1–10 (explain why)
2. List 3 competitive strengths
3. List 3 weaknesses or risks
4. Recommend ideal pricing
5. Suggest ways to enhance appeal
6. Give a go/no-go verdict

Step 3: Define Your Target Audience

Deep audience knowledge = better writing + marketing.

You are a Consumer Psychologist. Help me define my audience:

1. 2–3 detailed personas (demographics + traits)
2. Top 5 emotional pain points
3. Knowledge gaps they're trying to fill
4. Their buying process (what triggers action?)
5. Why they buy vs. consume free info
6. Language/tone/style that speaks to them

Part 2: Customize Your AI Writing Partner (30 minutes)

Create a Quick Style Guide

This ensures your eBook sounds like YOU, not a robot.

You are a Literary Style Analyst. Analyze this writing sample and create a style guide.

[Paste 250–500 words of your writing]

Please describe:
- Voice (casual, formal, confident, etc.)
- Sentence style + rhythm
- Vocabulary and tone
- Transitions + structure
- Explanation or teaching approach
- Signature style elements

Test Your AI Voice Alignment

See if ChatGPT can write in your tone:

You are my Personal Writing Collaborator. Using our style guide:

Rewrite the following in my authentic voice:
[Paste a short paragraph]

Explain how you applied my tone + where to improve.

Part 3: Draft Your Sales Page (60 minutes)

Start with the end in mind. Drafting your sales message early aligns your content with your reader’s goals.

You are a Direct Response Copywriter. Create key sales page components:

1. 5 headline options
2. 8 benefits that speak to my readers’ pain
3. A strong emotional problem statement
4. Who this eBook is for / not for
5. Unique selling proposition
6. Justify the value
7. First draft of a guarantee

Part 4: Set Up a Success Tracker

Keep track of progress, pacing, and quality across the 7 days.

You are a Digital Product Launch Manager. Build a tracker:

- Daily milestones (Day 0–7)
- KPIs (market readiness, confidence, clarity)
- Quality checkpoints (review, edit, flow)
- Red flags (where people quit)
- Progress system (checklists or ratings)
- Minimum thresholds (word count, completion)

✅ Final Checklist Before Day 1

  • ✔️ Writing space set up + 7-day schedule planned
  • ✔️ eBook idea validated through research
  • ✔️ Target audience clearly defined
  • ✔️ Sales copy rough draft complete
  • ✔️ AI style guide created and tested
  • ✔️ Success tracker ready to update daily
