Day 0: Setup & Market Research

Welcome to Day 0 of the 7-Day eBook Writing Challenge! Today is all about laying a strong foundation so you’re ready to move fast with clarity. You’ll validate your idea, prep your tools, and get your success tracker in place.

Today’s Focus

  • Clarify and validate your eBook idea
  • Research your market and ideal reader
  • Prep your AI tools and workspace
  • Create your success tracker

Why This Matters

Writing without direction leads to burnout and unfinished drafts. By the end of today, you’ll have a clear idea that’s aligned with your expertise, your audience, and real demand — setting you up for success over the next 7 days.

Quick Wins

  • Use ChatGPT or your AI tool of choice to validate your eBook concept
  • Look at Amazon categories to see how similar books are positioned
  • Create a folder for each day of the challenge on your device or in Google Drive
  • Download your editable success tracker

Prep Checklist

  • ✅ Chosen your eBook topic
  • ✅ Researched competing titles
  • ✅ Installed or accessed your AI tools
  • ✅ Opened your writing folder or doc
  • ✅ Saved the 7-day roadmap
