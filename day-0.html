Day 0: Setup & Market Research

Congratulations on beginning your 7-day eBook writing journey! Before you start writing, we'll set up your environment, train ChatGPT to match your voice, and validate that your eBook concept has real market potential. This preparation ensures a smooth writing process and an eBook positioned to sell.

Part 1: Market Validation (60 minutes)

Step 1: Analyzing Bestselling eBooks

Analyze the best-selling eBooks in your niche to understand what's working in the market.

You are an eBook Market Research Specialist with 15 years of experience. I want to create an eBook about [your topic].

Please conduct a comprehensive market analysis:
1. List top 5 bestselling titles, authors, and selling points
2. Analyze language patterns in descriptions
3. Note price, page length, bonuses
4. Highlight gaps in the market
5. Provide insights on trends and positioning

Step 2: Evaluate Commercial Potential

Now evaluate whether your specific eBook idea has commercial potential.

You are a Digital Product Launch Strategist. Based on the above, evaluate my eBook idea:

1. Rate viability 1–10 with reasons
2. List 3 strengths vs. bestsellers
3. Flag 3 potential weaknesses
4. Recommend price range
5. Suggest 3 market appeal enhancers
6. Give go/no-go recommendation

Step 3: Identify Your Target Audience

Define clear personas to guide your writing and marketing strategy.

You are a Consumer Psychologist. Help me create a detailed audience profile:

1. 2–3 personas (demographics, behaviors)
2. 5 pain points
3. Current knowledge and gaps
4. Buying journey and motivators
5. Tone, style, and language preferences

Part 2: Customizing Your AI Writing Partner (30 minutes)

Step 1: Style Guide Development

Paste a writing sample and generate a custom style guide with this prompt:

You are a Literary Style Analyst. Analyze my writing sample:

[Paste your writing]

Return a style guide with:
- Voice (casual/formal, etc.)
- Sentence structure patterns
- Vocabulary preferences
- Transitions and flow
- Explanation/teaching techniques

Step 2: AI Voice Testing

You are now my Personal Writing Collaborator. Using our style guide, rewrite this paragraph in my voice:

[Paste your paragraph]

Explain how the style guide was applied.

Part 3: Draft Your Sales Page (60 minutes)

Crafting a sales page early clarifies your value proposition and sets your eBook up for success.

You are a Direct Response Copywriter. Based on my concept, draft:

1. 5 headline options
2. 8 benefit-driven bullets
3. Emotional problem statement
4. Who this is for / not for
5. Unique selling proposition
6. Value justification
7. Guarantee statement

Part 4: Success Metrics Dashboard

Create a metrics dashboard to track your goals, writing milestones, and overall progress.

You are a Digital Product Launch Manager. Create a dashboard that includes:

- Daily milestones (Day 0–7)
- Commercial KPIs
- Quality checkpoints
- Red flags
- Progress tracker
- Success thresholds

✅ Final Checklist Before Day 1

  • Writing space and 7-day schedule ready
  • Market validation complete
  • Target audience profile documented
  • Initial sales page elements drafted
  • AI writing voice tested
  • Success tracker created
