7-Day eBook Writing Challenge

Here’s What You’ll Get

Daily Roadmap

AI Prompt Packs

Structure & Support

You'll follow a proven framework with the option to reach out if you need help.

What You’ll Walk Away With

  • A complete eBook draft ready for formatting and publishing
  • A repeatable system you can use again and again
  • Confidence in using AI as a creative tool, not a shortcut
  • A topic aligned with your expertise and your audience’s needs

Is This Challenge Right for You?

This is for you if:

  • You have a story, solution, or message to share
  • You want to write a useful, well-structured eBook quickly
  • You prefer guidance over guessing
  • You’re ready to commit 1–2 hours per day for 7 days

This may not be for you if:

  • You want AI to do all the writing for you
  • You’re looking for a done-for-you ghostwriting service
  • You’re not ready to start or finish a writing project

Do I need to have a book idea already?
No. We’ll help you choose the right idea on Day 0 based on your goals.
