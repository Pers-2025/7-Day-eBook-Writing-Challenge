Day 7: Publishing & Launching Your eBook

Congratulations! You've built a strategic, value-driven eBook. Now it's time to publish, promote, and position it to sell—today and into the future.

Step 1: Choose Your Publishing Platform

Choose a platform based on your audience and goals: Amazon KDP, Gumroad, Google Play, Draft2Digital, or your own site. Match platform strengths to your marketing capabilities and desired royalty control.

Ask ChatGPT

You are a publishing strategist specializing in eBook revenue optimization. Based on my market positioning, recommend the ideal publishing platform...

Step 2: Price Your eBook Strategically

Use price as a positioning tool. Consider premium, value-based, or penetration pricing depending on your niche and goal. Test different price points and observe conversion rates to find your sweet spot.

Ask ChatGPT

What is the optimal pricing strategy for my eBook if I want to balance authority and accessibility in a moderately competitive niche?

Step 3: Write a High-Converting Sales Page

Your sales page should lead with transformation, highlight benefits, handle objections, and include a strong call to action. Use buyer psychology and structured messaging to convert interest into sales.

Ask ChatGPT

You are a conversion copywriter. Create a high-converting sales page for my eBook titled [Your Title] that targets [Your Audience] and solves [Primary Problem]...

Step 4: Build Your Author Profile

Your bio should reinforce credibility, highlight results, and align with your audience’s values. This builds trust and increases perceived value of your work.

Ask ChatGPT

You are a personal branding expert. Create a strategic author bio based on my credentials and experience that resonates with [Target Audience]...

Step 5: Plan Your 7-Day Launch

Don’t just launch—strategize. Warm your list, share transformation-driven content, and use urgency and social proof to build momentum throughout the week.

Ask ChatGPT

You are a book launch strategist. Build a 7-day custom launch plan for my eBook with daily actions, social media content, and metrics to track...

Step 6: Build Systems for Long-Term Sales

Implement systems for gathering reviews, analyzing performance, collecting testimonials, and generating passive traffic. This ensures ongoing visibility and income.

Ask ChatGPT

Create an ongoing sales system for my eBook. Include review generation, content repurposing workflows, analytics dashboards, and optimization checklists...

Final Checklist Before You Launch

  • ✔ Platform selected based on your audience and goals
  • ✔ Sales page and author bio are live and optimized
  • ✔ Price point tested and adjusted for conversion
  • ✔ Launch campaign content is scheduled and ready
  • ✔ Follow-up and review systems are in place

Final Reflection

You've written, positioned, and prepared to launch your eBook with clarity, strategy, and heart. You're not just an author — you're a digital creator with systems to scale. Well done!
